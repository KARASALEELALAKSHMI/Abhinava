Electrical Installation Services by Abhinava Associates

Abhinava Associates offers comprehensive electrical installation solutions that adhere to Indian Standards, ensuring safe and efficient power systems. Our team handles wiring, earthing, load calculations, and panel setups, guaranteeing optimal performance and compliance.

We prioritize safety and quality, ensuring every aspect of the installation meets high standards. From design to execution, our team delivers exceptional results, reducing operational risks and improving efficiency.

Our electrical installations cater to industrial, commercial, and residential clients, providing reliable and efficient power systems. By partnering with us, clients receive exceptional service and support, trusting their electrical systems are in good hands.

IS-Compliant Electrical Solutions

Delivering safety-focused installations following Indian Standards (IS) for long-term reliability.

Wiring as per IS 732

Safe, code-compliant wiring systems designed according to IS 732 to prevent faults and hazards.

  • Minimized risk of shocks and short circuits
  • Optimized power flow and installation layout
  • Durability and quality assurance

Earthing System as per IS 3043

Secure your electrical systems with earthing networks built as per IS 3043 guidelines.

  • Reliable fault current discharge
  • Enhanced equipment safety and uptime
  • Protection against lightning and surges

Load Calculation as per IS 3961

Ensure optimal system sizing with IS 3961-compliant load analysis and planning.

  • Eliminate overload risks
  • Optimize transformer and feeder capacities
  • Future expansion compatibility

Conduit and Cable Routing

Structured routing systems for organized and protected electrical pathways.

  • Reduces cable clutter and interference
  • Ease of maintenance and upgrades
  • Mechanical and environmental protection

Panel & Switchgear Setup

Design and deployment of distribution panels and switchgear for safe load control.

  • Reliable circuit protection and switching
  • Compact and modular design options
  • Customization for industrial and commercial use

Inspection & Testing as per IS 16406

Validate your installation’s integrity with thorough testing and certification services.

  • Identifies installation flaws and risks
  • Confirms standard compliance
  • Reduces downtime and safety issues
